Lake Association Manager

A web application created for a lake-side community transitioning into an Association in Spring 2024. It facilitates democratic processes through proposal voting and user submissions. The collective decision-making helps shape community governance and fosters ownership and inclusivity among its members.

With an aim to make community members' lives easier it allows them to manage tasks remotely, freeing up more time for leisure activities at the lake. It features an event calendar to keep everyone informed about upcoming activities. Moving forward, the focus is on expanding the application with additional social elements, enhancing engagement and connectivity.

Dwel

A web based application designed to be a central hub for homeowners to keep track of maintainence and repair tasks associated with their property(s). Users can create an account, add properties to their profile, upload an image of the property, add tasks and equipment associated with a property, and manage the completeness of tasks.

This was my first major group project working with a real world client and other developers. It helped enhance my skills in communication, time management, and prioritization.
